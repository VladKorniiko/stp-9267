 Puzzle Mechanics

How Do You Conquer a World Made of Candy?

  • Match 3 candies to pop them off the board
  • Match 4 or 5 to unleash Super Candies
  • Clear jelly, ice, chocolate, and waffle blockers
  • Activate boosters like lollipop hammers and sprinkle bombs
  • Chain reactions = mega sugar blasts
  • The fewer the moves, the higher your score

It's easy to start, but tricky to master — just like the perfect lollipop swirl.

      FAQ

      • Is Lollipop World free?

        Yes! Completely free, with optional in-app items.

      • Are there lives or timers?

        No timers. Lives refill over time or by watching a quick ad.

      • Can I play offline?

        Yes – all main levels are playable offline.

      • How many levels are there?

        Over 2,000, with new ones added every two weeks!

      • What do I do when stuck?

        Use boosters, ask friends for lives, or retry with a fresh strategy.
