About the game

A Candyland Classic Reimagined

  • Lollipop World is one of the most charming and satisfying match-3 experiences available.

  • With over 2,000 handcrafted levels, new candy mechanics every few worlds, and a gentle learning curve, this is a treat for newcomers and veterans alike.

  • Switch, match, and explore - with no timeer, no pressure, and no limits to the sugar fun.
